The Volunteer Musketeers!

Hello! We are a group of four girls (Irene Chen, Soliana Getachew, Maura Riordan, and Sabrina Ventoso) who want to make volunteering around the country easier for everyone, especially students. These days, with so many things on our minds, it's hard to organize and keep track of where and when you've volunteered, in addition to how many hours you already have and how many you still need. To appease this problem, we have created this prototype of a website that gives you a sense of how our actual website would be run, and what would be on it. Feel free to take a look around!
